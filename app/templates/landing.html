How It Works

One Voice Note. That's It.

No app. No form. No username. No password. Just talk to WhatsApp the way you talk to your customers.

🎤

1. Send a Voice Note

"Alhaji Sule carry 2 cartons milk for ₦20,000. Him number na 0807…"

🧠

2. AI Understands

PayWise transcribes your voice, extracts the name, amount, goods, and phone number — in your language.

🏦

3. Debtor Gets Account

A real bank account number is generated and sent to your debtor. They pay from any bank app.

💰

4. You Get Paid

When money lands, your wallet updates instantly. Both parties get WhatsApp receipts. No manual work.

Features

Built for the Market, Not the Boardroom

🗣️ Voice-First

Send voice notes in your natural speech. No typing required. Works even on basic phones with WhatsApp.

🌍 5 Nigerian Languages

Speak Pidgin, Yoruba, Hausa, Igbo, or English. PayWise replies in the same language — real dialect, not Google Translate.

🏦 Real Bank Accounts

Every debt gets a unique bank account number via Nomba. Debtors pay from any Nigerian bank app. No USSD. No payment links.

⚡ Instant Settlement

When a debtor pays, your wallet balance updates in real time. Both you and the debtor get WhatsApp receipts simultaneously.

📒 Never Lose a Sale

Started recording a debt but forgot the phone number? PayWise saves it as a draft. Complete it later. Nothing is ever lost.

🔒 Trust by Design

The AI proposes changes — only YOU confirm them. The system physically cannot write to your ledger without your "yes."
