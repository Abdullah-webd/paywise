Why it matters

The market runs on trust. PayWise gives trust a record.

Traders do not need complicated finance software. They need a simple way to remember who bought what, who has paid, and what money is actually theirs.

5:47 AM

Mama Bola opens her shop and checks yesterday's notebook. One smudged amount can mean money she never recovers.

12:20 PM

Three customers collect goods on credit. Names, numbers, prices, and due dates are easy to forget when the shop is busy.

With PayWise

She sends one voice note. PayWise extracts the sale, asks for confirmation, creates the payment route, and keeps the ledger clean.

How it works

One conversation. A complete credit record.

The flow stays where traders already are: WhatsApp. PayWise handles the accounting structure quietly behind the scenes.

01

Send a voice note

Speak naturally in Pidgin, Yoruba, Hausa, Igbo, or English.

02

Confirm the details

PayWise reads the customer, item, amount, number, and due date back to you.

03

Get a payment account

A Nomba virtual account is created so the debtor can pay from any bank app.

04

Track your money

Your wallet, debts, and transaction history update when payments arrive.
