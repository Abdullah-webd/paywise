The Story

Meet Mama Bola.

She sells provisions at Oyingbo Market, Lagos. This is her day. This is why we built PayWise.

5:47 AM. Mama Bola opens her shop. She pulls out a worn notebook — 47 names, scribbled amounts, crossed-out numbers. She squints. "Alhaji Musa... ₦8,500 or ₦5,800?" The ink is smudged. She can't tell. That's ₦2,700 she'll never recover.

By noon, three more customers take goods on credit. She writes their names on a receipt she'll probably lose before Friday. Her daughter helps after school, but neither of them speaks accounting. The bank won't give her a loan — she has no credit history. She's been running this shop for 14 years.

This is not just Mama Bola's story. It's the story of 27 million Nigerian market traders who move billions of naira — tracked on paper, managed from memory, lost to chaos. They don't need an MBA. They don't need QuickBooks. They need something that speaks their language.

How It Works

One Voice Note.
That's It.

No forms. No apps. No typing. Just open WhatsApp and talk. PayWise does the rest.

Step 1
🎤

Send a Voice Note

"Alhaji Musa buy 3 bags rice for ₦45,000. He go pay next week. Him number na 0803..."

Step 2
🧠

AI Understands You

PayWise transcribes your voice in Pidgin, Yoruba, Hausa, or Igbo — extracts name, amount, goods, phone number.

Step 3
🏦

Debtor Gets a Bank Account

A real Nomba virtual account is created. Your debtor gets an SMS with the account number. They pay from any bank app.

Step 4
💰

You Get Paid. Instantly.

Money lands → wallet updates. Both you and your debtor get receipts. No manual work. No chasing people.

Features

Built for the Market,
Not the Boardroom.

🗣️

5 Nigerian Languages

Speak Pidgin, Yoruba, Hausa, Igbo, or English. PayWise replies in YOUR language with proper grammar and native-sounding voice.

🎙️

Voice Note Replies

Long replies come as voice notes. Each language has a unique persona — Aunty Bose, Iya Gbemi, Mama Ngozi, Hajia Aisha.

📒

Never Lose a Sale

Forgot the phone number? PayWise saves it as a draft. Complete it later. Nothing is ever lost — even mid-conversation.

🏦

Nomba Virtual Accounts

Every debt gets a real bank account number. Debtors pay from any Nigerian bank app. Settles instantly via webhooks.

💳

Wallet & Withdrawals

Live balance, transaction history, bank withdrawals — all from your secure wallet dashboard. AI never touches money.

🔒

Trust by Design

The AI proposes — YOU confirm. No write without your yes. Withdrawals require password on the web dashboard.
